Featured Article: Advances in CRISPR Gene Editing

Researchers have made significant strides in precision gene editing using CRISPR-Cas9 technology. New delivery mechanisms are enabling targeted therapies for previously untreatable genetic disorders, bringing hope to patients with conditions such as sickle cell disease and beta-thalassemia.

Research Spotlight: Gut Microbiome and Mental Health

A growing body of evidence links the gut microbiome to brain health and mood regulation. Recent clinical trials suggest that targeted probiotic interventions may help alleviate symptoms of anxiety and depression, opening a new frontier in neuropsychiatric treatment.

Industry News: mRNA Technology Beyond COVID-19

Building on the success of mRNA COVID-19 vaccines, biotech companies are now developing mRNA-based therapies for cancer, rare diseases, and other infectious diseases. Several candidates are currently in Phase II clinical trials with promising early results.
