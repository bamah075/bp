# #06 Full Marketing Campaign Launch

**Use case:** Coordinated campaign across email, social, ads, and landing page — with cross-channel consistency review.

---

Create an agent team to build a complete marketing campaign for a product launch.

**The product:** A premium noise-canceling headphone called "FocusPods Pro" priced at $249, targeting remote workers and creators who need deep focus in noisy environments. Key selling points: 40-hour battery life, spatial audio for virtual meetings, a "Focus Mode" that filters out human voices but lets through doorbells and alarms, and a built-in Pomodoro timer with haptic taps. Launching on our DTC website with a 30-day money-back guarantee.

Spawn 4 teammates:

1. **Email Marketer** — Write a 3-email launch sequence:
   - Email 1 (teaser, 2 days before): Build anticipation, subject line that gets opens
   - Email 2 (launch day): Announce FocusPods Pro, 3 key benefits, clear buy-now CTA
   - Email 3 (follow-up, 2 days after): Social proof angle, "first 100 buyers get a free carrying case" urgency
   
   Each email: subject line, preview text, body (150-250 words), CTA button text. Save to outputs/agent_teams_demo/marketing_campaign/email_sequence.md.

2. **Social Media Manager** — Create platform-specific launch content:
   - LinkedIn post (150-250 words, professional angle: productivity and remote work)
   - Twitter/X thread (7 tweets, hook first, CTA last)
   - Instagram caption (lifestyle angle, short and punchy, hashtag strategy)
   - TikTok script (30-second hook: "POV: your coworker won't stop talking during your deep work block")
   
   Save to outputs/agent_teams_demo/marketing_campaign/social_content.md.

3. **Ad Copywriter** — Write 3 variations of ad copy:
   - Variation A: Problem-agitation-solution
   - Variation B: Social proof / results ("87% of beta testers reported deeper focus in week 1")
   - Variation C: Us-vs-them comparison (AirPods Max vs FocusPods Pro feature table framing)
   
   Each variation: headline (max 40 chars), body (max 125 chars), CTA text, targeting suggestions. Save to outputs/agent_teams_demo/marketing_campaign/ad_copy.md.

4. **Landing Page Writer** — Write the full copy for a product landing page:
   - Hero section (headline + subheadline + CTA + press logos)
   - Product feature blocks (Focus Mode, 40hr battery, Spatial Audio, Pomodoro Timer)
   - Social proof section (3 placeholder testimonials)
   - Comparison table (FocusPods Pro vs AirPods Max vs Sony WH-1000XM6)
   - FAQ section (5 common objections)
   - Final CTA section with urgency
   
   Save to outputs/agent_teams_demo/marketing_campaign/landing_page_copy.md.

After all 4 finish, review all content for: consistent messaging and tone, no contradictory claims, same core value proposition in every piece, CTA links all point to the same destination.

Save a campaign summary with messaging guidelines to outputs/agent_teams_demo/marketing_campaign/campaign_summary.md.
